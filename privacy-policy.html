Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Wrapped ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Summary: We only collect your Spotify listening history to show you personalized stats. We never sell your data or share it with third parties for advertising.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Wrapped, we collect the following information through your Spotify account:

  • Spotify Profile Information: Your Spotify user ID and display name
  • Listening History: Tracks you've played, including song names, artist names, album information, and timestamps
  • Playback Data: Duration of listening sessions and play counts

We do not collect:

  • Your Spotify password
  • Payment or billing information
  • Your location data
  • Contacts or other personal files

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your listening data solely to:

  • Display your personalized listening statistics and insights
  • Show your top artists, tracks, and listening trends
  • Generate charts and visualizations of your music habits
  • Sync and store your history so you can view it across sessions

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers with the following protections:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely in iOS Keychain
  • Database access is restricted and protected
  • We do not store your Spotify password

4. Third-Party Services

Wrapped connects to the following third-party services:

  • Spotify: We use Spotify's official API to access your listening history with your permission. Spotify's use of your data is governed by Spotify's Privacy Policy.

We do not share your personal data with advertisers, analytics companies, or any other third parties.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view all data we have about you within the app
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us
  • Revoke Access: You can disconnect Wrapped from your Spotify account at any time through your Spotify Account Settings
  • Logout: You can log out of the app at any time, which removes local authentication tokens

6. Data Retention

We retain your listening history data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • All your personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymized, aggregated statistics may be retained

7. Children's Privacy

Wrapped is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
